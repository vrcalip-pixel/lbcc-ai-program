Course Description

AI and Machine Learning Foundations

This course introduces students to foundational Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) principles, preparing them for workplace applications in business, marketing, education, and healthcare. Topics include AI history, types of AI, machine learning basics, real-world applications, and ethical considerations. Students will analyze AI-powered tools and develop skills in evaluating AI technologies for responsible and effective use in professional environments.

Tools you'll work with: ChatGPT · Claude · Google AI · IBM Watson · Microsoft Copilot · Data visualization tools

What You'll Learn

By the end, you'll be able to —

  1. Differentiate AI, machine learning, and deep learning — and explain where each shows up in business, healthcare, and education.
  2. Examine the real impact of AI technologies, including algorithmic bias, privacy concerns, and ethical challenges you'll encounter at work.
  3. Evaluate real-world AI applications and industry trends to assess their effectiveness and their limitations.
  4. Demonstrate proficiency in using AI-powered tools for data analysis, automation, and digital assistance in workplace scenarios.
  5. Develop strategies for responsible AI integration in business and organizational settings.
  6. Assess AI-driven decision-making tools to determine their benefits and challenges in professional applications.
