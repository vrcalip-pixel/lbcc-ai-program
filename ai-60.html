Course Description

Applied Conversational & Agentic AI

This course introduces students to the design and application of conversational and agentic AI systems. Students learn how modern AI models enable natural language interactions, multi-turn dialogues, and autonomous task completion. Using no-code and low-code platforms, learners will design, test, and deploy conversational assistants, workflow agents, and domain-specific AI applications. Emphasis is placed on human-centered design, ethics, and responsible deployment across industries such as business, education, customer service, and healthcare. Students produce a portfolio project showcasing a functional AI agent or assistant.

Tools you'll work with: Voiceflow · OpenAI Assistants API · Claude Projects · LangChain (low-code) · Google Dialogflow · Microsoft Copilot Studio

What You'll Learn

By the end, you'll be able to —

  1. Define the key concepts in conversational and agentic AI — intents, entities, orchestration, memory, and autonomy.
  2. Diagram conversation flows and agent workflows, including error handling and escalation logic.
  3. Apply user-centered design to build inclusive and accessible AI interactions.
  4. Build and test conversational and agentic prototypes with platforms like Voiceflow, OpenAI Assistants, and LangChain-style frameworks.
  5. Conduct usability tests and interpret analytics to iteratively refine agent designs.
  6. Explain ethical, privacy, transparency, and bias concerns related to autonomous AI systems.
  7. Present a capstone agentic AI project with documentation and reflection.

Introduction to Conversational & Agentic AI

Core Concepts

  • Conversational memory and context

8 hours
III

Conversation & Workflow Design

  • Flowcharting and task decomposition
  • Human-in-the-loop design
  • Ambiguities, clarifications, fallback logic
7 hours
IV

Ethics, Inclusivity & Governance

  • UDL principles in AI interactions
  • Bias, fairness, inclusivity in language
  • Privacy, security, transparency
5 hours

Hands-On Agent Building

  • Agents for FAQ, scheduling, support, automation
  • Context management and workflow chaining
10 hours

Deployment & Integration

  • Reliability and compliance

Testing & Analytics

7 hours
